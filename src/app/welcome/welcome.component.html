Welcome to Quiz Arena!

This is a fun quiz app to test your general skills. Begin this quiz only when you are prepared to complete it. You may refer to your friend or google for assistance.

Quiz Rules:

  1. The quiz Consists of 10 questions.
  2. You have one minutes to answer each question. Once the time is up the question is considered incorrect.
  3. Each correct answer gives you 10 points.
  4. Incorrect answers deduct 10 points from your existing score.
  5. To pass the quiz, you need to score a minimum of 60 points.

All the best! Goodluck.
